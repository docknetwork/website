Governance is a vital element of building a decentralized network and Dock is committed to iterating towards a decentralized state.

Voting center

We believe that our community should guide the direction and development of Dock. This voting center is setup to facilitate community-wide voting proposals. We will regularly post polls here about decisions we are making at Dock and ask you to vote on your preferred option.

Note: DOCK tokens are required to vote

This process is meant to be the first of several steps towards embracing decentralized governance for the betterment of the Dock network and community. DGP-2: Move Dock from Ethereum to Polkadot

Since Dock's inception the market has experienced many advancements in technology that enable faster development velocity, vastly improved scalability, on-chain governance upgrades, and far better user experiences. In this pivotal and exciting time, Dock Core Developers are preparing to release an updated lightpaper after alternative blockchain ecosystem choices have been evaluated.
